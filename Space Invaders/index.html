Space Invaders

Rules

  • Protect Earth from the alien Invaders!
  • Pilot your space craft and shoot down the alien invaders as they decend from space.
  • Every Alien you shoot is worth 10pts!

  • If an alien gets past your defense, you lose 1 life!

  • If you get hit by an alien's bullet, you lose 1 life!

  • If you loss all three lives, its GAME OVER!

Controls

  • A and D: To Move Left or Right
  • Space Bar: Shoot Alien Invader
